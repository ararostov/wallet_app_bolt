Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Retail Wallet ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information: email address and profile data you provide during registration.
  • Financial data: transaction history and wallet balances associated with your account.
  • Device information: device type and OS version for technical support purposes.

2. Camera Permission

The app requests access to your device camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes to facilitate payments and transfers. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never stored or transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality.
  • To process transactions and display your account information.
  • To communicate important updates about your account.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to provide core app functionality (e.g., payment processing partners).

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
